When you can't find the words — let your emotions speak through art

Art therapy method based on the idea that the creative process can help people explore their emotions, develop self-awareness, and find new ways to understand themselves and resolve problems.

The Petals Concept

Imagine that a person is a flower. Petals are emotions — multi-layered, sometimes chaotic, sometimes harmonious.

Fresh & Alive Petals

Some emotions are new, vibrant, and full of life — these help us grow and flourish.

Withered & Tired Petals

Others are worn, tired, and have outlived themselves — these often prevent us from growing further.

Every Emotion Matters

Each emotion is fragile, changeable, and unique. Together they form a whole — you.
